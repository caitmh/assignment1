Projects

  • Air Diffusion Systems

    Air Diffusions Systems is a 3rd generation company committed to providing its clients with reliable, efficient, and highly effective water and wastewater aeration equipment. My role at ADS is to develop and grow the company's online presence through multiple social media platforms as well as researching and writing case studies and graphic design.

  • Building a Better Colorado

    IVC developed and implemented the digital program and online outreach strategy for the Building a Better Colorado Coalition which held over 40 summit meetings to discuss reforming Colorado's fiscal policy, election system, and constitutional amendment process. IVC worked with the Building a Better Colorado Coalition to target, reach, and foster an online and offline community engaged in the decision-making process in Colorado. My role was to create, edit, and advertise educational content for Building a Better Colorado's social media pages.

  • Colorado Families for a Fair Wage - Amendment 70

    Colorado Families for a Fair Wage is a coalition of small business owners, community partners, working families, and faith organizations working together to help build a fair economy for all Coloradans by passing Amendment 70. The coalition worked to increase Colorado's minimum wage to $12 by the year 2020. Amendment 70 passed with 55 percent support in Colorado. My role was to develop the campaign's online social presence to foster online and offline community engagement, oversee the implementation of the campaign's digital advertising program, web development, and graphic design.

  • Fix My Energy Bill

    IVC created an award-winning website and conducted a campaign in support of California Assembly Bill 327 in 2013. IVC continues to educate California voters through the Fix My Energy Bill brand online. My role was to create, edit, and advertise educational content for Fix My Energy Bill's social media pages.

  • Independent Voter Network

    IVN News is an online, nonpartisan news outlet dedicated to raising the level of civil discourse to a place where solutions are more persuasive than talking points. IVN believes that democracy functions best when the most people participate. I published over 150 articles focusing on nonpartisan election reform with over 170,000 online interactions.

  • Let Colorado Vote

    Let Colorado Vote ran two ballot propositions for the 2016 election cycle. Propositions 107 and 108 re-established the presidential primary system and opened primary elections to the state's 1.3 million unaffiliated voters. IVC worked with Let Colorado Vote to develop their social media presence and online community. My role was to create and edit educational content for Let Colorado Vote's social media pages.

  • Rancho Santa Fe Post

    IVC developed and maintains the Rancho Santa Fe Post, an online community newspaper and Facebook page dedicated to educating and increasing community engagement in Rancho Santa Fe, California. My focus was to create and edit content for the online newspaper, newsletter, and Facebook page.
